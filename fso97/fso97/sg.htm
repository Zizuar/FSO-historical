WHAT IS A SIM?

A sim is where a group of people get together and act out parts, that they are given or that they want. You take parts such as Engineer, Helm, Tact etc. Than you act it out. The Captain will sometimes tell you what to do, or you can ask he or she if it is alright to do what you think might be fun and intersting. Also as time goes on you will get promted. You will start out as a Cadet than work your way up into higher ranks. If you are intersted in simming please Join today

WHAT DOES EACH PERSON  DO IN A SIM?

  • Captain

The Captain is the creator the story teller of a sim. He or She will write up a sim and than play it out through there crew. The Captain will tell you what to say or do sometimes, but most of the time they don't need to, you can do whatever, as long as it is not ridiculous.

  • First Officer

The first Officer (XO) is just that. . . He/she oversees all departments, and has the authority to give orders to anyone on the ship, except of course, the Captain. He/she is responsible for delegating work schedules and seeing that all maintenance schedules are kept. He develops and organizes the duty roster. The first officer will lead away teams, unless otherwise specified by Captain.

  • Medical

The Chief Medical Officer (CMO) is of course the Doctor of the ship. The CMO is responsible for the physical well being of the crew. The CMO will also perform studies on alien and unknown life forms. And will give medical assistance to anyone or anything that needs it.

  • Engineering

The engineering section is run by the Chief Engineer, who is responsible for all personal within Engineering. Engineering handles nearly all mechanical aspects of the ship, but first and foremost is the warp and impulse engines. They are also responsible for the power requirements of the ship. Engineering also does nearly all the diagnostics and maintenance of the ships systems.

  • Science

Science is responsible for all scans, reports, and other types of pertinent data on all types of encountered phenomenon. Conducts surveys and studies of same. A science officer is on all away teams.

  • Operations Management (OPS)

The OPS officer is responsible for coordinating ship activities so as to not overload any systems. Also handles communications, hailing, ship to ship, away teams, etc. OPS also handles all transporter operations, and monitors away teams status. OPS fills in for Helm when necessary.

  • Tactical

Tactical is responsible for all defensive systems (shields, phasers, photons, etc.) And internal security. Tactical also scans and provides data on all encountered vessels.

  • Security

Is responsible for the security of the ship and all persons aboard. A security person shall accompany all away teams. Security Chief reports to Tactical Officer.

  • Helm

Helm is responsible for all flight control operations of the ship, course plotting, manual flight and position verification.

  • Counselor

The Counselor is responsible for the mental health of the crew. He or she will provide counseling to all persons aboard the ship. He or she will also give special guidance and insight to the Captain, by providing the Captain with psychological profiles and opinions.

  • Chief Intelligence Officer

Is responsible for being able to gather information on various cultures, weapons, or technology that a ship may encounter. Must go on all away missions and is also responsible along with the Chief Security Officer for the well being of the away team. Can also handle communications and scans of other vessels. The Intelligence Officer reports directly to the Tactical Officer.
