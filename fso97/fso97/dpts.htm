Research and Development:
The Research and Development Department approves all Ships and Equipment/Technology on the ships. This also includes Space Stations and Colonies etc. All requests for the above must be approved by Admiral Quest. Drawings of ships and such is handled by Admiral Quintano and also DarkLord and Suvon, who are Quest's assistants.
Public Relations:
Public Relations Department does ALL aspects of communications with other Sim Groups. Admiral Quest heads up this department. It handles all merger and aquisition communcations/negotiations with other sim groups and ships.
Race Department:
NPC Race Department handles all npc race encounters in all FSO sims. Admiral Quest along with Admiral Quintano head up this department that approves or rejects encounters with NON official, authorized Star Trek or Star Wars publications.
