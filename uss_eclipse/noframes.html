Greetings, and welcome aboard the USS Eclipse Website. The USS Eclipse is a RPG (Role Playing Game), also known as a SIM (short for simulation), that is in the Federation Sim Organization. The Commandering Officer, is currently Captain Jonathan Paris.
The Federation Sim Organization (FSO) is a Sim group that has sims including, Star Wars, Star Trek, Babylon 5, and much more. There are links for most of the FSO Sims and the FSO homepage on our links page. The USS Eclipse Sim takes place in the year 2376, during the Deep Space Nine timeline.
