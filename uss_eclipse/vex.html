Lieutenant JG Vex

NAME: Vex
RANK: Lieutenant Junior Grade
CURRENT ASSIGNMENT: Chief Tactical Officer
SHIP: USS Eclipse, NCC-80381
GENDER: Male
AGE: 50 earth years
BIRTHDATE: June 15, 2326
BIRTHPLACE: Earth, North Carolina
HAIR COLOR: Dark Blonde
EYE COLOR: Purple-Bluish
DESCRIPTION: Height - 6 ft. 3in. Weight - 165 lbs. Sleek but muscular
PERSONALITY: Suave, but direct and straight forward, open, considerate, friendly, stern at times, religious dedicated person.

PERSONAL/FAMILY HISTORY:
Born on Earth, to parents Sharon and Phillip Grange. Lived on Earth for 18 years, during which time he entered StarFleet academy and graduated with Top Honors. He left Earth and had a prime role in a genetic experiment gone wrong. Using borg technology and Earth Genetic enhancements, Grange was transformed into partially borgified-genetically enhanced person. His brain motor functions were not effected but endurance and strength as well as speed were enhanced about 3 times the normal for a Human. Vision and hearing all enchanced about twice a normal human capacity. Extraoridary components for long-life has been adapted. A Technical Vision attachment is worn over the right-eye and some arm mechnical enhancements.

HISTORY IN STARFLEET:
First commanded the USS Victory which was later decommissioned after it's 10 year service. Served on Starbase Kylon along the Cardassian border during the Maquis engagements. Fell in love with a maquis captive and later helped her to escape and return to her friends and "homeland" with the Maquis. Reprimanded by starfleet and placed on record. Later met up with the Maquis associate named Kassandra Orives. They married and she entered StarFleet academy. After graduating she requested a commission on the StarBase but was denied. She was sent to USS Mekenza where she became Lt. Commander after 5 year service. Her first command ended up badly, as CO of USS Benadix, during the Borg Wolf 359 encounter, her ship was destroyed. Lieutenant Vex was then assigned to command the new Galaxy Class StarShip Labrith but refused.
The stress from his wife's death gave him reason to take leave on some beautiful planet for a while. After 4 years, he was assigned to the USS Eclipse as the Chief Tactical Officer.
