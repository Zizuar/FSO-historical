Welcome to the Official Federation Sim Organization Website. First we'll tell you a bit about FSO.
The Federation Sim Organization, also known as the FSO, is a Sim/RPG Group. Most of our sims are SciFi related, but we always welcome Non-SciFi Sims. The Majority of our sims take place on IRC, on the Superchat Network. We also have Post Board sims, EMail Sims and a MUSH.
