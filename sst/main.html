The Starship Troopers sim is a Live IRC based sim that takes place in the Starship Troopers universe, shortly after the movie. There are currently two parts to the sim, the U.S.S. Calypso (SS510) and the Roughnecks, lead by Commander Mish.
The Roughnecks are part of the Mobile infantry. They are a special elite group who specialize in Bug Killing *grin*. Please look around our website and Sign Up to become a Citizen!!!!
