Paris was born in the Pennsylvania area of North America on the 19th day of May in 2235. Paris followed his father's footsteps by becoming a dreamer, an astronomer, and began to show interest in becoming a Pilot. There was an Air Force Base in nearby New Jersey, so after graduating from schooling, Paris departed home and went to New Jersey for training. He did good in training and learned fast. He was assigned to the USS Athene after completing his training. Several years later, he was assigned to USS Calypso.
