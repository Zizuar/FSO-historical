Star Trek: The Galactic War - Rules






 Each planet provides natural resources.
These resources can be anything from man-power, to raw materials, to training grounds for troops. As a way of simplifying resources, all types of resources will be generalized into Resource Points. These Resource Points will be used in the construction and repair of ships, shipyards, and planetary defenses and will represent everything from the raw materials needed, to troops trained to run the ship, to training grounds for the troops.
The number of resources a planet produces depends on the planet.
These resources are used to pay for new ships.
Once a ship is paid for, it must be built at a shipyard. The building time depends on the size of the ship.
Only the faction leaders can build ships.
