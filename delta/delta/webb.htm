CREW RECORDS: Webb, Jonathan

Name: Jonathan Webb (III)
Age: 19
Former Commander of the StarBase 297, however after going through a temporal anomoly, was trapped in the AlterVerse where he de-aged until he was 14.

With the help of Lt Cdr Jason Janson, Webb was re-aged to 19, until the shuttle could not handle any more temporal stress. Because he was de-aged however, SFI decided to demote him.
