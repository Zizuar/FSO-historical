On September 19, 1999, President Cody Kyle Felton resigned from the office of President.  As a result, an election has been called for October 15-18, 1999.   The new president will have a life term, as Felton did.  Each FSO member (who has been a member for at least 1 month) is entitled to ONE vote, regardless of how many email addresses they have or how many sims they are on.  Votes will be accepted from 12:00pm ET Friday October 15, to 12:00pm ET Monday October 18.  Any member of FSO who is Lieutenant Commander or above may run, providing they are nominated by 1 FSO member each.  The nominations will occour in teams of 2 (eg. 2 ppl run together for Pres. and VPres.).

To nominate someone, email election@fsosim.net saying:

I, (your name) of (your sim), do hereby nominate (pres candidate) to run for President, along with (vpres candidate) as his/her vice presidential candidate.   I hereby state that I have not nominated any other candidates, and am not a candidate or electoral officer.

Signed,

Your name.

Any invalid nominations will be rejected, and anyone who attempts to nominate more than one candidantial team will result in all of their nominations will be revoked.

Once the nominee's have accepted, they must send a candidational profile to BOTH webmaster@fsosim.net and election@fsosim.net. This is where you talk about yourselves and your platform.  There is to be NO public campaigning (and no /msging strangers either), so please use your page as your campaign.  If you are caught campaigning publically, you WILL be disqualified.  This is not a popularity contest.
